Made to measure

Each sink is planned around the architecture of the room: width, depth, tap position, storage, surface finish and the way the piece meets surrounding tiles or joinery.

Why Bespoke

A porcelain sink should feel designed into the room, not added at the end.

A bespoke porcelain sink gives freedom over scale, edge treatment, finish and the surrounding details. It can sit quietly within a calm bathroom scheme or become the defining architectural element of a vanity wall.

We use precise planning and careful installation to create pieces that feel light, durable and composed, with joins and transitions handled as part of the design.

Best Uses

Where bespoke porcelain sinks work best

A made to measure porcelain sink is ideal when a standard basin does not suit the proportions, layout or finish of the room. We create bespoke porcelain sinks for private bathrooms, cloakrooms, vanity walls, guest bathrooms and refined hospitality interiors across London.

Each piece can be planned around the available width, depth, tap position, storage needs and surrounding wall or floor finishes, so the sink feels integrated rather than added afterwards.

02 — Process

Process

From first dimensions to a finished porcelain piece.

The process is calm and measured: we gather the practical details first, then refine the proportions, finish and installation plan before fabrication begins.

  1. 01

    Enquiry

    Share the room, rough dimensions, location and any drawings or photos you already have.

  2. 02

    Design direction

    We advise on proportion, material, edge style and how the sink should integrate with the surrounding surfaces.

  3. 03

    Specification

    Dimensions, finish, basin layout, details and installation requirements are resolved before work begins.

  4. 04

    Making & fitting

    The sink is fabricated and installed with attention to alignment, joins and everyday usability.

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently asked questions

Can a porcelain sink be made to fit a specific space?

Yes. Each sink can be planned around the room dimensions, basin layout, tap position, storage needs and surrounding surfaces.

Can you create a double vanity porcelain sink?

Yes. Longer porcelain compositions can be designed for double vanity areas, depending on the size of the room and the required basin layout.

Do you work with existing bathroom designs?

Yes. We can work from photos, measurements, drawings or an existing design direction and advise on the most suitable proportions and finish.
