Privacy

Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Artiling Studio handles the details you share when enquiring about bespoke porcelain sinks and surface work, and how website analytics help us understand and improve enquiries.

Your Details

How we handle enquiry information.

Information We Collect

When you contact us, email the studio, or complete a quote request, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, preferred contact method, project location, project type, project notes, measurements, budget range, timeline, and any photos or files you choose to share.

How We Use It

We use your information to review your enquiry, understand your project, respond with next steps, prepare relevant guidance or estimates, arrange follow-up communication, and keep practical records of enquiries and projects.

Form Processing

Quote form submissions are processed through Web3Forms, which helps deliver the details you submit to Artiling Studio. Please only upload photos or files that you are comfortable sharing for the purpose of reviewing your enquiry.

Analytics, Cookies and Similar Technologies

The website uses Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity to understand how visitors use the site, which pages are useful, and where enquiries may need a clearer path. These tools may use cookies or similar technologies to collect information such as pages visited, approximate device and browser details, interactions with the website, and general usage patterns.

Analytics information is used in aggregated form to improve the website, understand enquiry journeys, and make the site clearer for future visitors. Microsoft Clarity may record anonymised interaction data such as clicks, scrolling and page behaviour to help identify usability issues.

Advertising and Conversion Measurement

If Artiling Studio runs online advertising, analytics and conversion measurement may be used to understand which pages and campaigns lead to enquiries. This helps us improve the website and measure advertising performance. We do not use this information to sell personal details.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share details where needed to respond to your enquiry, support project planning, provide website and form functionality, understand website performance, or meet legal obligations.

Retention

We keep enquiry information for as long as needed to manage the enquiry, maintain project records, provide follow-up support, and support reasonable business administration. If an enquiry does not become a project, we may still keep a practical record for a limited period so we can understand past conversations and avoid losing useful context.

Your Rights

You can ask us to correct, update or delete enquiry information we hold about you, unless we need to keep certain details for legitimate business records, legal reasons, or an active project. You can also ask how your enquiry details are being used.

Contact

To ask a privacy question, request a correction, or ask for deletion of your enquiry information, email info@artilingstudio.co.uk.
