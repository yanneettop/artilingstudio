Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Artiling Studio handles the details you share when enquiring about bespoke porcelain sinks and surface work.

Your Details

How we handle enquiry information.

Information We Collect

When you contact us or complete a quote request, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, project location, project notes, measurements, and any photos you choose to share.

How We Use It

We use your information to review your enquiry, understand your project, respond with next steps, and prepare relevant guidance or estimates.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share details where needed to respond to your enquiry, support project planning, or meet legal obligations.

Retention

We keep enquiry information only for as long as needed to manage the enquiry, maintain project records, and support reasonable business administration.

Contact

To ask about your personal information, email info@artilingstudio.co.uk.
