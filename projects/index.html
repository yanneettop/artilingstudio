Bespoke Porcelain Sinks

A bespoke porcelain sink allows the vanity area to be planned as part of the architecture, rather than treated as a separate fixture. We consider scale, fall, tap position, splashback height, storage, edge detail and the way each surface meets the wall or surrounding joinery. For London bathrooms where every line is visible, this level of planning gives the finished piece a calmer, more resolved presence. Our bespoke porcelain sink service is suited to private bathrooms, cloakrooms, guest suites and premium interiors that need a tailored finish.

Large Format Tiling

Large-format tiling is most effective when the layout feels effortless. Behind that simplicity is careful substrate preparation, accurate setting out, considered cuts and controlled transitions around corners, niches, thresholds and vanity areas. Porcelain slabs and large tiles can make bathrooms, floors and feature walls feel more spacious, but only when proportion and alignment are handled with restraint. Our work with large-format tiling and surfaces focuses on clean planes, reduced visual noise and durable finishes for refined residential and commercial spaces in London.

Wet Rooms & Bathroom Tiling

Wet rooms and bathroom tiling require a balance of technical preparation and visual discipline. Falls, waterproofing, drainage, tile format, grout position and fixture alignment all influence the final result. We plan these details early so the finished space feels composed, practical and precise. Whether the brief is a calm shower room, a full porcelain bathroom or a feature vanity wall, Artiling Studio works with clients and design teams across London to create bathrooms where surface, proportion and function are handled as one.

Porcelain Details & Material Studies

Material studies help refine the language of a project before installation begins. We look at porcelain tone, vein direction, edge thickness, mitred corners, shadow gaps, lighting, brassware and the relationship between horizontal and vertical planes. These details are subtle, but they define whether a bathroom or feature space feels truly considered. If you are developing a porcelain surface project, sink commission or premium tiling scheme, contact Artiling Studio to discuss the material direction and next steps.
